The Color Match Platform

A real-world Self-Driving Lab powered by IvoryOS.

The Deployed Platform

This platform was deployed to automate the process of color matching. It uses a robotic arm to orchestrate the entire workflow, moving samples between different stations.

The system iteratively tests different ratios of primary colors, measures the result, and uses an optimizer to suggest the next best recipe.

Troubleshooting Bayesian Optimization

Optimizing the color mixture is driven by Bayesian Optimization, but it is not magic. There are limitations to what the optimizer can achieve without the right setup.

Sometimes the optimizer fails to reach the target color difference and the learning curve plateaus. When this happens, a common troubleshooting step is to expand the search space.
